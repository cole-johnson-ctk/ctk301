Retention Requirements

Sequence in Audio and Music Production and Interdisciplinary Technologies: Students will be allowed to continue in this sequence if they meet and maintain an overall cumulative GPA of 2.50.

Sequence in Game Design: Students must maintain a cumulative GPA of 2.50 overall and 3.0 within the major.

Standards for Progress in the Major

Students must maintain a cumulative GPA of 2.50 and demonstrate competency through an intermediate portfolio review.

Sequences in Creative Technologies

Accelerated Creative Technologies Sequence

High achieving students with a cumulative GPA of 3.20 or higher may apply to the accelerated sequence one year before completion of their undergraduate degree.

Audio and Music Production Sequence

This sequence provides concentrated study in sound recording, audio production, sound design, and music composition.

Game Design Sequence

This sequence provides concentrated study in game design and production.

Sequence in Interdisciplinary Technologies

This sequence provides concentrated study in mobile, web, and computer programming concepts.
